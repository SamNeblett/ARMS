GENERAL INFORMATION

Client:
NASA
System Overview:
ARMS (Astrounaut Resource Managing System) is an application that allows austronauts to visualize available resources related to its own safe condition during mining operations and to keep them always under control. In case of excessive, unexpected, in other words WRONG consumption of one of the available resources, the system activates requested recovering operations both automatically and manually.
Goals and Objectives of the Project:
- Monitoring Resources - Keep safe astronauts
Possible Users for the System:
Astronauts
Hardware and Software Requirements:
- OLED Display - Astronaut Resource Monitoring App
Hardware and Software Requirements:
- OLED Display - Astronaut Resource Monitoring App
